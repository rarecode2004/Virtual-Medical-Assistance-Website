Order Your Medicine

This application has a feature that can track the availability of the prescribed medicine in nearby medical shops through the GPS (Global Positioning System), where the patient does not need to spend time on various medical shops about the availability. It will suggest the user nearby medical shops, if not they are supposed to buy they can order that particular medicine in this same application itself. In case of any emergency situation, they can also track the nearby hospitals, where immediate care is needed.

Cardiology

Atenolol

Atenolol is a beta-blocker medication primarily used to treat high blood pressure (hypertension), angina (chest pain), and certain heart conditions. It works by blocking beta-adrenergic receptors, which slows down the heart rate and reduces the heart's workload

Lisinopril

Lisinopril is an angiotensin-converting enzyme (ACE) inhibitor used to treat high blood pressure, heart failure, and improve survival after a heart attack. It works by relaxing blood vessels, making it easier for the heart to pump blood efficiently

Hydrocortisone Cream

Hydrocortisone cream is a topical corticosteroid used to reduce inflammation, redness, and itching caused by various skin conditions such as eczema, dermatitis, insect bites, and allergic reactions. It works by suppressing the immune response in the affected area, thereby alleviating symptoms of swelling and irritation.

Carbamazepine

Carbamazepine is a prescription medication used to treat epilepsy, trigeminal neuralgia, and bipolar disorder. It works by affecting nerve signals in the brain, helping to reduce excessive electrical activity that leads to seizures and nerve pain

Sumatriptan

Sumatriptan is a prescription medication used to treat migraines and cluster headaches. It belongs to a class of drugs called triptans, which work by narrowing blood vessels in the brain and reducing inflammation to relieve headache symptoms
